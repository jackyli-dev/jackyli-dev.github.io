## Overview

This is a small escape game where you have to find passcodes to unlock five doors.

It was hard to find assets to make our ideas come true. After 10 hours of not getting anywhere, we decided to grab a simple background image and make a puzzle game. We carefully designed the puzzles. Hope you enjoy!
